Craft materials can be expensive and hard to source. Here are links to some of our society's most regularly used businesses to help you find what you need.

HobbyCraft

HobbyCraft isn't the cheapest but they have an easy to navigate website that does deliveries and a big store in Union Square. As a chain store it means you're guaranteed to find what you need regardless of what craft you're shopping for.


The Works

The Works is a cheaper chain compared to places like HobbyCraft but it's range can be quite limited, especially when it comes to sewing. They have a shop in Trinity Centre.


Amazon

Make use of your Student Prime and cheap prices. When in doubt, Amazon probably has it in 5 different colours.


Wool Warehouse

Wool Warehouse is the most popular place in the society. Despite its name it caters to all craft forms and is in a more affordable price range to HobbyCraft
