Welcome to Pins & Needles!

PolarPins & Needles Society is RGU's unofficial society for all crafters! We knit, embrioder, crochet and more! Several events for all styles of crafts are organised throughout the year for all to enjoy - whether you are just starting out or have perfected your craft.


Crafting Society Wins Awards

RGU's crafting society 'Pins & Needles' wins Aberdeen Non Academic Society of the Year, narrowly beating their own university's RetroGames Society & Aberdeen University's Swimming Society. This is the first award Pins & Needles has won since forming only 2 years prior, and their Event Organiser and co-founder Pati Knapczyk states that it was well deserved and that the society has truly flourished in the past year with their members almost doubling.
